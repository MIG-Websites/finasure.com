Who We Are

A Holding Group Built on Trust & Compliance

FinAsure is a Gulf-based financial services holding group with a focused portfolio in fintech and insurtech. We operate at the intersection of financial innovation and regulatory integrity, building companies that serve real needs across the MENA region.

With dual headquarters in Manama, Bahrain and Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, FinAsure bridges two of the region's most dynamic financial hubs. Our structure enables subsidiaries to scale within robust regulatory frameworks, with oversight from both the Central Bank of Bahrain (CBB) and the Saudi Central Bank (SAMA).

We don't just invest — we build, govern, and grow companies that are engineered to last.

Our Vision & Pillars

The Principles That Drive Us

Every decision, every investment, every company we build is guided by three core pillars that define what FinAsure stands for.

01
Inclusion

We build products and platforms that extend financial and insurance services to underserved individuals and businesses across the Gulf — making access the rule, not the exception.

02
Innovation

We back founders and build ventures that leverage technology to solve deep, structural challenges in financial services — with a bias toward bold, scalable solutions.

03
Impact

Our mandate goes beyond returns. We measure success by the economic value we create for the people, businesses, and communities our companies serve across the region.
